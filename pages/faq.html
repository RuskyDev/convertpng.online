Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I convert my PNG image to another format?

To convert your PNG image to another format, simply upload your image by clicking 'Choose File,' select the desired format from the dropdown menu, and then click the 'Convert & Download' button. The converted image will be available for download immediately.

2. What formats can I convert my PNG image to?

You can convert your PNG image to various formats including JPG, BMP, WEBP, TIFF, and ICO. Simply select the format you want from the dropdown menu before converting.

3. Is there a file size limit for the images I can upload?

Currently, there is no strict file size limit, but larger files may take longer to process. If you encounter issues with very large files, try resizing them before uploading.

4. How secure is my uploaded image?

Your uploaded image is processed directly in your browser, so you don’t need to worry. We don’t store or keep your image on our servers everything is processed locally in your browser.

5. Can I contact support if I have further questions?

Yes, if you have any further questions or need assistance, you can contact us via email at iamayaanalee@gmail.com. We will be happy to help!
